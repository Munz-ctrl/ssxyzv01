Our mission

IRDS works with vulnerable Sudanese communities to reduce suffering and increase self-reliance through practical, locally-led programs.

Locally rooted

Registered in Sudan and operating across Darfur and other states, IRDS partners with community leaders and local authorities to design realistic, sustainable interventions.

Evidence-based programs

From small business development to animal health and WASH, our work builds on more than a decade of field experience with UN and NGO partners.

Climate & livelihoods together

The Plant a Tree initiative adds a climate lens to our livelihood programs, helping communities adapt to droughts, soil degradation and displacement.

Why trees?

Trees protect farmland from wind and sand, restore soil, provide fruit and shade, and create dignified local jobs for youth and women.

How it works

Donors select a package, IRDS plants trees through existing field projects, and communities care for them. We report back with simple updates and photos.
